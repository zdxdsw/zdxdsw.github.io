Several Times In The Margin

I just finished a gorgeous Nietzsche chapter: Of the flies of the market-place.
Yesterday I got a Kierkegaard book from a used book store.
I read one chapter that spoke to me a lot (The Greatest Danger). I thought I had found gold. ✌️
Then I read another chapter (Either/Or) that didn't speak to me at all.
Angry, and even more intrigued by such lottery-like reading,
I opened Thus Spoke Zarathustra.
It begins: "FLEE, my friend, into your solitude!"
What a seamless and great continuation!
So I just finished a gorgeous Nietzsche chapter
where I couldn't help but writing “I LOVE YOU!!” several times in the page margin.
